Privacy Policy for Hyphn

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us at Hyphn. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our generative large language model AI web application.

Information We Collect

When you use our AI web application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • User Inputs: Text prompts and queries submitted to the AI for generation or analysis.
  • Generated Outputs: Responses generated by the AI based on your inputs.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the application (e.g., pages visited, features used, timestamps).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and other technical details necessary for the application's functionality.

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it in your inputs or account information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to provide and improve the functionality of the AI web application. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Generate responses based on your inputs.
  • Enhance the accuracy and quality of the AI model.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the user experience.
  • Monitor and ensure the security and performance of the application.

Your inputs and generated outputs may be used to further train and improve the AI model, but only in a de-identified and aggregated form.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Storing data on secure servers with encryption.
  • Implementing strict access controls to protect sensitive information.
  • Regularly updating security protocols to safeguard against vulnerabilities.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except under the following circumstances:

  • When required by law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our application, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your data will never be sold or used for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Control: You can request access to your inputs and outputs, and you may request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out: You can choose not to have your data used for model training and improvement.
  • Inquiries: Contact us with any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your data.

Cookies and Tracking

Our application may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.
